Corn Bread

Description

A Soul Food staple and long lasting recipe that has been remixed and passed down by my family. You get a sweet and soft golden cake thats not only tasty but will leave you full!

Ingredients

  • Cornbread Box Mix (trust me)
  • 3 Tbl Spoons of Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup of Half & Half Milk
  • 1 Tbl Spoon of Sour Cream
  • 1 Tbl Spoon of Butter
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and stir very well. The consistency shouldnt be watery but not very thick. Add more milk if too thick .
  2. Butter a small pan and pour the mix. Make sure to spread the mix evenly.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees for 30-40 minutes.
  4. Remove when its a nice golden brown color and spread butter on the top
  5. Let it sit for 5-10 mins and then enjoy!
